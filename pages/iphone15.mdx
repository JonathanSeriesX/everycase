# iPhone 15 case lineup

On September 13th, 2023, Apple announced iPhone 15 series. There's not much to say about it; it's just another iPhone. But among other things, this Apple Event marked Apple's move from leather accessories to new material called FineWoven, as part of the push for sustainability. And it did not go well...

## In the Wild

Customers were quick to voice their displeasure with new FineWoven Case.

## Pricing / Availability

iPhone 15 Silicone Case and Clear Case cost $49, while FineWoven cases cost $59.

## Compatibility

iPhone 15 series features curvier corners than previous models, so cases made for iPhone 15 series are only compatible with iPhone 15 models.
