# Docking Stations

Before Apple's higher-end chargers learned to float onto the back of the phone, they were little cradles that held the phone upright on your desk.

All these docks have a 3.5 mm audio line-out, so a docked iPhone could pipe sound straight into powered speakers while it charged and synced.

They do not support fast charging from USB-C power adapters.

## iPhone Lightning Dock
