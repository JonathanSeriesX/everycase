# iPhone 17 case lineup

The 2025 lineup finally patched the premium-case gap left by FineWoven's demise: please welcome TechWoven. Apple also came up with a whole new way to carry your iPhone — the [Crossbody Strap](/others/crossbody).

### Compatibility

iPhone 17 cases are not compatible with previous models.

Non-Beats iPhone 17 cases are compatible with [Crossbody Straps](/others/crossbody).

As with every iPhone since the 12, both the phones and their cases work with [MagSafe Wallets](/others/wallet).
