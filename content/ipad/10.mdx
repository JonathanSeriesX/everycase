# Accessories for Liquid "just" iPads

By the end of 2022, Apple decided to further complicate iPad line-up; thus, this device was born…

But, we're here to talk about accessories, aren't we?

### Compatibility

These are only compatible with iPad (10th generation) and iPad (A16).

## Magic Keyboard Folio

Unlike the standard Magic Keyboard, this one features a detachable keyboard section.

## Smart Folio

Apple refreshed the four original Smart Folio colours with new order numbers for iPad (A16), likely without changing the accessory itself.
