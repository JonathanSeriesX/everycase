# iPhone 15 case lineup

On September 13th, 2023, Apple announced iPhone 15 series. There's not much to say about it, but this Apple Event also marked Apple's move from leather accessories to new material called FineWoven, as part of the push for sustainability.

## In the Wild

## Pricing / Availability

iPhone 15 Silicone Case and Clear Case cost $49, while FineWoven cases cost $59.

## Compatibility

iPhone 15 series features curvier corners than previous models, so it's only compatible with iPhone 15 cases.
