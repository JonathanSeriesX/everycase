# Accessories for Liquid "just" iPads

By the end of 2022, Apple decided to further complicate iPad line-up; thus, this device was born…

But, we're here to talk about accessories, aren't we?

## Pricing & Compatibility

Smart Folio costs $79, while Magic Keyboard Folio will set you back $249. Accessories for iPad (10th generation) also fit iPad (A16).

## Magic Keyboard Folio

Unlike the standard Magic Keyboard, this one features a detachable keyboard section and includes an F-row. This keyboard solution is exclusive for iPad (10th gen).
