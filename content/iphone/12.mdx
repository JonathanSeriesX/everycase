# iPhone 12 case lineup

On October 13, 2020, Apple broadcasted a virtual event (remember COVID? Yeah, those times) dedicated to the iPhone 12, the HomePod mini, and a few other exciting announcements.

For the first time in human history, we got a phone you could actually hold comfortably in your hand.
I still cherish my iPhone 12 mini to this day—it’s a gem of a device.

And this time, both the mid-range and flagship models received full case treatment. Pretty solid event!

### Compatibility

These cases are not compatible with similar-looking iPhone 13, just like iPhone 12 is not perfectly compatible with iPhone 13 cases.

iPhone 12 also introduced MagSafe, so it's the first iPhone to work with [MagSafe Wallets](/others/wallet).
