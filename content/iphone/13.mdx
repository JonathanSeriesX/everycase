# iPhone 13 case lineup

Another year, another iPhone lineup, another bunch of cases. Pretty nice colours, too; Wisteria is one of my all-time favourites, it's just so nice. No more Leather Sleeves tho ):

### Compatibility

iPhone 13 models feature a slightly bigger camera bump, so they are not compatible with cases made for the iPhone 12.

Both the phones and their cases work with [MagSafe Wallets](/others/wallet).
