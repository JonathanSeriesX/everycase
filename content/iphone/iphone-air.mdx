# iPhone Air case line-up

The iPhone Air arrived in 2025 with its own line of slim cases and bumpers. Its accessories are made specifically for the iPhone Air and do not fit other iPhone models.

## Pricing & Compatibility

The iPhone Air Case is priced at \$49, the Bumper at \$39, and the Beats Case at \$45.
