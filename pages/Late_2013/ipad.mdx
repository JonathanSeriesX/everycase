# Accessories for iPad Air & mini 2

On November 1, 2013, Apple released two very, very good iPads: iPad Air & iPad mini with Retina Display.
iPad Air's new design was mostly inspired by last year's iPad mini, and it was reused in some way right until release of 10th generation 'just iPad' in Late 2022.

Both iPads recevied an accompanying collection of polyurethane Smart Covers and leathers Smart Cases.

## Pricing / Availability

Smart Covers for both iPads retailed for $39; iPad Air Smart Case costed $79, while iPad mini Smart Case costed $69 (nice!).

iPad Air Smart Case is only compatible with iPad Air. All later 9.7" iPads had different dimensions.

Even though iPad mini 2 was 0.3 mm thicker than predecessor, all iPad mini accessories are interchangeable between iPad mini 1, 2 and 3, but not later models.
