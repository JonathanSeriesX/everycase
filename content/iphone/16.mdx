# iPhone 16 case lineup

The iPhones just keep on coming, don't they. Good news: no more FineWoven. Bad news: no premium kind of cases either. Pretty sure Apple will fix that gap in 2025... _Update:_ they [did](/iphone/17#techwoven-case).

### Compatibility

iPhone 16 Pro models feature a different size compared to 15 Pro, and iPhone 16 features a different camera layout compared to 15, so these cases won't fit previous models and vice versa.

Both the phones and their cases work with [MagSafe Wallets](/others/wallet).
